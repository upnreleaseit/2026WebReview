About Me

Get to know the developer behind the code

I grew up in Canton, New York — a small town along the Grass River in St. Lawrence County, just a short drive from the St. Lawrence River and the northern edge of the Adirondacks. I've always been curious about how things work, and that curiosity led me straight into computer science. There's something deeply satisfying about writing a few lines of code and watching a browser spring to life.

Through the SLLBOCES Computer & Information Science program, I've gone from writing my first <h1> tag to building interactive JavaScript applications and thinking critically about the ethics of technology in our society. After graduation, I plan to pursue a degree in computer science or software engineering — and maybe come back to build something that helps the North Country community I grew up in.
